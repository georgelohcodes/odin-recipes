Chicken Curry Recipe

Description

Indulge in the rich flavors of this delectable chicken curry. It's a perfect dish to savor with friends and family, offering a tantalizing blend of tender chicken, aromatic spices, and creamy coconut milk. To make this mouthwatering curry, begin by marinating chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt and spices. In a large pan, sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant, then add the marinated chicken and brown it. Add a medley of spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala, followed by diced tomatoes and coconut milk. Simmer until the chicken is tender and the sauce is rich and flavorful. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan for a truly satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tsp cumin powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the yogurt, half of the minced garlic, half of the grated ginger, and a pinch of salt. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions, remaining garlic, and ginger. Sauté until the onions turn translucent.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and brown it on all sides.
  4. Stir in the cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Cook for a few minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk and simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens.
  7. Season with salt to taste and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve hot with rice or naan. Enjoy your homemade chicken curry!
